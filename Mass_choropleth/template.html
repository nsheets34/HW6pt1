Homework 6 Part 1: Census data choropleth

Nicholas Sheets

This map displays a choropleth of housing data in Massachusetts. This dataset shows housing by displaying the number of occupied housing units that are in each of Massachusetts' 14 counties. This project helps to show the distribution of housing and population in Massachusetts. Although imperfect due to factors such as homelessness and land area, this gives us a rough estimate of more rural and urban counties within Massachusetts.
